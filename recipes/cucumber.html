California Roll Cucumber Salad

This California roll cucumber salad is easy: mix sliced cucumber, diced crab, diced avocado, seaweed, rice vinegar, soy sauce, pickled ginger, and sesame seeds in a glass jar to make this TikTok viral salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 5 imitation crab sticks, diced
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • 2 sheets seaweed, torn into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped pickled ginger
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Directions

Step 1

In a pint canning jar with a lid, add sliced cucumber, crab, avocado, seaweed, rice vinegar, soy sauce, pickled ginger, and sesame seeds. Cover tightly and shake until all ingredients are combined.
